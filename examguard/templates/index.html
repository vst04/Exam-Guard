What are the smart capabilities of the software?

AI Monitoring

AI monitoring detects cheating by analyzing head movements, hand gestures, and posture changes using object detection and body coordinates. It identifies suspicious behavior and ensures fair exams with real-time alerts.

Instant Alerts

AI monitoring instantly alerts proctors to suspicious behavior, including unusual movements or unauthorized device usage, enabling quick intervention and maintaining exam integrity by preventing cheating and ensuring a fair testing environment.

Reports

Frequently Asked Questions

What is an Exam Monitoring System?

A cheating detection system is an advanced technological solution designed to identify and prevent dishonest practices during exams, assessments, or tests. This system utilizes various methods such as AI-based video analysis, facial recognition, and behavioral tracking to detect suspicious activities like unauthorized resource usage, impersonation, or unusual movements. By ensuring a fair and secure testing environment, it helps maintain the integrity and credibility of academic evaluations.

How does an exam proctoring system identify cheating?

Exam monitoring systems use video recording and AI analysis to track the number of students present and detect any suspicious behavior that may indicate cheating or rule violations. These systems can automatically flag potential issues and notify instructors or proctors for further review.

How does the Exam Guard exam monitoring system work?

Exam Guard is an AI-powered exam monitoring system that uses computer vision techniques like MediaPipe and YOLOv5 to detect cheating in exam halls through CCTV surveillance. The system processes live video feeds to analyze student behavior, identifying suspicious actions such as looking away frequently, using unauthorized devices, or communicating with others. When cheating is detected, the system generates alerts and logs the event in a database for review. The collected data helps in maintaining exam integrity and provides a detailed report for invigilators to take necessary actions.

What technologies does Exam Guard use for cheating detection?

Exam Guard utilizes advanced computer vision and AI technologies, including MediaPipe and YOLOv5, to analyze real-time video feeds from CCTV cameras. MediaPipe helps in detecting facial landmarks and gaze direction, while YOLOv5 is used for object detection to identify unauthorized devices or suspicious actions. These technologies work together to monitor student behavior, detect potential cheating attempts, and generate automated alerts and reports for further review by exam invigilators.
